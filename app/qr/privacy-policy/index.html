Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 2, 2026

Overview

'QR Simple Generator', aka 'QRSG', is a simple QR code generator app and nothing more. No data is collected.

Data Collection

'QRSG' does not collect, store, transmit, or share any personal information.

  • No account registration
  • No analytics
  • No advertising
  • No tracking
  • No data is sent to any server

QR Code Generation

All QR codes are generated locally on your device using the open-source library QRCode by dagronf. No data leaves your device.

Third-Party Services

QRSG does not use third-party analytics, advertising frameworks, or tracking tools.
